The PrintAirPipe - 125 AirFlowSensor Segment is designed to measure moved air volume and airflow velocity inside a 125mm ducting system. The electronics use an ESP32-S3, three LEDs and Hall sensors. Power is provided through USB-C, and propeller speed is visualized externally via animation.
