## 🎯 How to Use

### Step-by-Step Assembly Guide

1. **Gather Your Materials**
   - Purchase all components from the Bill of Materials section above
   - All Amazon links are pre-configured with affiliate tags to support Nerdiy.de development
   - For STL files, [download from Printables](https://www.printables.com/model/1444123-raspberry-pi5-angled-mount-for-raspberry-pi-gs-or)

2. **Download 3D Files**
   - [🎨 Download from Printables](https://www.printables.com/model/1444123-raspberry-pi5-angled-mount-for-raspberry-pi-gs-or) (free download)
   - Alternative: Check [Nerdiy.de Shop](https://nerdiy.de/) for availability

3. **Prepare for 3D Printing**
   - Print the mount, AI HAT bracket, and camera bracket parts with these settings:
   - Layer Height: 0.2mm
   - Infill: 20-25%
   - Supports: Yes (for overhangs > 45°)
   - Material: PETG (recommended for durability and outdoor use if needed)
   - Slice and prepare files in your slicing software

4. **Assembly**
   - Clean all printed parts after removal from build plate
   - Install M2 threaded inserts into designated holes using soldering iron
   - Mount the Raspberry Pi 5 onto the base mounting plate
   - Attach the AI HAT to the Raspberry Pi 5 GPIO header
   - Mount the camera module to the angled bracket
   - Connect the camera ribbon cable to the Raspberry Pi 5 CSI port
   - Secure all parts with M2x20 screws
   - Verify camera angle and AI HAT connections

5. **Installation**
   - Mount the complete assembly in your desired location
   - Ensure proper ventilation around the Raspberry Pi 5 and AI HAT
   - Connect power supply (USB-C power input - ensure sufficient power for AI HAT)
   - Boot up your Raspberry Pi 5 and test camera and AI HAT functionality
   - Configure camera settings and AI HAT in Raspberry Pi OS or your preferred software

6. **Maintenance**
   - Periodically clean camera lens and housing from dust
   - Check screw tightness after extended use
   - Verify camera ribbon cable and AI HAT connections remain secure
   - Monitor AI HAT temperature during operation
   - Test camera and AI functionality regularly
